The SUV trend in India has crossed over into the electric segment. Buyers want high command positions, ample ground clearance, and spacious cabins, combined with silent and clean EV drivetrains. 

Here is our curated list of the top electric SUVs you can buy in India today.

## Top Electric SUVs Today

### 1. Mahindra BE 6
Mahindra's brand-new INGLO platform vehicle features futuristic styling and premium performance specs.
- **Battery Pack:** 60 kWh to 79 kWh
- **Drivetrain:** Rear Wheel Drive
- **Best For:** Enthusiasts wanting performance and striking designs.

### 2. Tata Nexon EV
The market-leading compact SUV that started the EV revolution. It features premium tech, a comfortable ride, and multi-mode regeneration.
- **Battery Pack:** 30 kWh to 40.5 kWh
- **Claimed Range:** 325 km to 465 km
- **Best For:** Balanced urban commutes and occasional highway runs.

### 3. Tata Curvv EV
With a coupe-SUV silhouette, the Curvv EV offers a larger boot and an extensive feature list.
- **Battery Pack:** 45 kWh to 55 kWh
- **Claimed Range:** up to 502 km
- **Best For:** Premium buyers seeking styling and segment-first features.

## What to Look For in an Electric SUV
When buying an electric SUV, ground clearance is crucial. Make sure the battery pack has a heavy-duty underbody shield to protect it from bad speed breakers. Also, look for SUVs that offer at least 50 kW DC fast charging capabilities so you can recharge quickly on family road trips.
