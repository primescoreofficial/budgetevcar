Electric vehicles (EVs) are no longer a luxury segment in India. Thanks to aggressive pricing from brands like Tata Motors and MG JSW, Indian consumers can now purchase feature-rich, high-range electric cars for under ₹15 Lakh. 

In this guide, we break down the top models available today that offer the perfect balance of budget, range, and daily usability.

## Why Go Electric Under ₹15 Lakh?
The sub-15 Lakh segment is the sweet spot for city commuters. Daily running costs are roughly **₹1 to ₹1.5 per kilometer**, compared to **₹6 to ₹8 per kilometer** for petrol cars. Furthermore, maintenance is minimal since EVs have fewer moving parts.

### Top EV Contenders

### 1. Tata Punch EV
The Tata Punch EV is built on the advanced acti.ev architecture. It offers a premium SUV stance, high ground clearance, and an impressive range.
- **Battery Pack:** 25 kWh to 35 kWh
- **Claimed Range:** 315 km to 421 km
- **Key Highlight:** 5-star Bharat NCAP safety rating and modern features like ventilated seats.

### 2. Tata Tiago EV
The Tiago EV remains India's most popular family hatchback. It is perfect for city runabouts with easy parking and tight turning dynamics.
- **Battery Pack:** 19.2 kWh to 24 kWh
- **Claimed Range:** 250 km to 315 km
- **Key Highlight:** Very competitive pricing starting under ₹8 Lakh.

### 3. MG Comet EV
If you want a pure city commuter, the MG Comet EV is a quirky, ultra-compact 2-door vehicle that is incredibly easy to drive.
- **Battery Pack:** 17.3 kWh
- **Claimed Range:** 230 km
- **Key Highlight:** High-tech cabin and easy maneuvering in heavy traffic.

## Summary Checklist
Before choosing your EV, determine your daily driving distance. For daily commutes under 60 km, smaller battery variants will save you money on the upfront purchase price. For longer weekend trips, prioritize larger battery packs that support fast DC charging.
