Global EV giant BYD has officially announced plans to introduce a new mid-size electric SUV in India. Positioned to compete directly in the premium mid-size EV segment, the new SUV will feature BYD's advanced Blade Battery technology.

## High Local Content Expected
BYD is reportedly exploring assembly options to price the vehicle competitively. By localizing key components, BYD aims to offer premium build quality and advanced range specifications at an aggressive price point.

The new model is expected to carry a certified range of over **500 km** on a single charge.
