MG JSW Motor India has announced a marginal price revision for its popular electric crossover, the Windsor EV, effective from next month. The price adjustment is accompanied by new ownership benefits designed to attract family buyers.

## New BaaS Packages Announced
The updated pricing structure includes new battery-as-a-service (BaaS) monthly packages. Buyers can now subscribe to battery usage at a lower per-kilometer rate, making the upfront acquisition cost of the Windsor EV even lower.

Current bookings will be honored at the introductory launch prices.
