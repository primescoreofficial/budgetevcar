Tata Motors has officially expanded its electric coupe-SUV lineup by launching a new mid-spec variant for the Curvv EV. Positioned between the entry-level Creative and top-spec Empowered trims, this new variant aims to make the Curvv EV more accessible to budget-conscious buyers.

## Premium Features at a Balanced Cost
The new variant comes equipped with a **45 kWh** battery pack, offering a certified range of up to **430 km** on a single charge. It also features standard DC fast charging capabilities that allow the vehicle to charge from 10% to 80% in 40 minutes using a 50 kW charger.

Inside, the cabin includes key upgrades such as a 10.25-inch infotainment screen, automatic climate control, and connected car features.
