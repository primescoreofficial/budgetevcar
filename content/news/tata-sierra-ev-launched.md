Tata Motors has officially launched the highly anticipated **Tata Sierra EV** in the Indian market, marking the return of one of the country's most legendary SUV nameplates in a cutting-edge electric avatar. Prices for the Sierra EV start at **₹18.79 lakh (ex-showroom)**, positioning it as Tata Motors' premium electric flagship. Bookings are officially open at authorized dealerships with a token amount of ₹21,000, and deliveries are scheduled to begin on **July 15, 2026**.

## The Legend Reborn: Exterior Design & Aesthetics

The design of the Tata Sierra EV is a masterful combination of futuristic clean aesthetics and nostalgia. Tata Motors has retained the iconic glass-house rear profile of the original 1990s Sierra, giving it a unique character. 

Up front, the Sierra EV features a full-width LED light bar with welcome and goodbye animations, a sleek blanked-off grille, and dynamic indicators. The side profile is highlighted by flush-fitting door handles, large aerodynamically optimized alloy wheels, and dual-tone roof options. The rugged appeal is enhanced by black body cladding, 204 mm of ground clearance, and functional roof rails. At the rear, a continuous LED tail light bar matches the front lighting signature, completed by a rugged skid plate.

## High-Tech Interior: The "Horizon View" Cockpit

Inside, the Tata Sierra EV offers a lounge-like premium cabin experience. The dashboard is dominated by the **Horizon View triple-screen cockpit**, which includes:
- A **12.3-inch touchscreen infotainment display** supporting wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto.
- A **10.25-inch fully digital instrument cluster** with navigation projection.
- A dedicated **passenger-side screen** for media control and connected app interactions.

Other interior highlights include:
- **JBL Premium Sound System:** A 12-speaker audio system with Dolby Atmos tuning.
- **AirConsole Integration:** Allowing passengers to play games on the infotainment screen using their smartphones as controllers.
- **Panoramic Sunroof:** Adding to the spacious and airy feel of the cabin.
- **Boot Space:** A massive **622-litre boot** which is expandable up to 1,257 litres with the rear seats folded flat.

## Battery, Performance & Charging Capabilities

The Tata Sierra EV is built on the advanced Acti.ev dedicated born-electric platform. It is offered with two Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) battery pack options:
1. **63 kWh Battery Pack (RWD):** Delivering a MIDC-certified range of **565 km**.
2. **75 kWh Battery Pack (RWD / AWD):** Delivering a MIDC-certified range of **665 km**.

The AWD variants feature dual motors (one on each axle) with six dedicated terrain response modes for off-road driving. The performance is brisk, with Tata claiming a 0–100 km/h acceleration time of under **5.8 seconds** in Boost Mode.

### Charging Speeds
The Sierra EV supports up to **120 kW DC fast charging**, allowing the battery to charge from **20% to 80% in just 26 minutes**. For home charging, Tata provides a complimentary 7.2 kW AC wallbox charger capable of a full charge in approximately 8 to 9 hours.

## Level 2+ ADAS & Safety Features

Safety is a core pillar for Tata Motors, and the Sierra EV is packed with advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) and active safety features:
- **Level 2+ ADAS:** Includes 22 active safety functions such as Adaptive Cruise Control, Autonomous Emergency Braking, Lane Keep Assist, Blind Spot Monitoring, and Rear Cross Traffic Alert.
- **540° HD Camera:** Offering a complete birds-eye view of the vehicle and its surroundings, including the underbody.
- **Airbags:** 6 airbags, Electronic Stability Program (ESP), and ISOFIX child seat mounts are standard across all variants.

The Sierra EV targets premium lifestyle SUV buyers who appreciate modern technology, off-road capability, and heritage design.
