A coalition of leading charge point operators in India has announced an ambitious infrastructure expansion plan to deploy over **5,000 new high-speed DC fast chargers** along major national highways over the next 18 months.

## Intercity Travel Simplified
The expansion targets busy highway corridors, including Mumbai-Pune, Delhi-Jaipur, and Bengaluru-Chennai. Chargers will range from **60 kW to 150 kW**, allowing long-distance travelers to quickly top up their batteries during short breaks.

This initiative is expected to significantly reduce range anxiety for EV owners planning intercity journeys.
