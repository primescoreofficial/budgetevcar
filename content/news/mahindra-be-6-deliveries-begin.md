Mahindra & Mahindra has officially commenced customer deliveries of the highly anticipated BE 6 electric SUV across major metro areas in India. Built on the brand's purpose-built INGLO born-EV platform, the BE 6 marks a major milestone in Mahindra's new electric vehicle strategy.

## First Batch Handed Over
The first set of deliveries was completed at premium dealerships in Mumbai, Delhi-NCR, Bengaluru, and Pune. Mahindra plans to scale up production rapidly to meet the massive pre-booking orders received during the launch phase.

According to dealer sources, waiting periods for the top-spec battery variants are currently hovering around 3 to 4 months.

## High Performance & Premium Styling
The Mahindra BE 6 features a futuristically styled coupe silhouette, premium cabin finishes, a heavy-duty battery package, and quick DC charging capabilities. Mahindra is also offering customers a complimentary 7.2 kW home wallbox charger and smart app connectivity options.
